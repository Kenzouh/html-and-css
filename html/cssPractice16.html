Notes: Pseudo-Elements aren't selectable. The pseudo-elements include ::first-letter(usually for magazines), ::first-line (coloring the first line or making the text bigger/smaller), ::marker (bullets), ::before (anything you wanna put before the first character), ::selection (when selecting texts, you can define the background color and the color of the text) and ::after (lets you put anything after the texts/content by using the "content: "";" property).
